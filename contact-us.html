Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Visit our website, browse through our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can also call us at +20 (2) 2791-5678 or use WhatsApp for assistance.

What is your delivery timeframe?

In Cairo: Same-day delivery available until 2 PM. Standard delivery takes 24-48 hours. Outside Cairo: 2-5 business days. All deliveries are tracked.

Do you offer prescription refills?

Yes! You can request prescription refills online through your account, via phone, WhatsApp, or by visiting our pharmacy in Downtown Cairo. Our licensed pharmacists process refills promptly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all credit/debit cards, Vodafone Cash, Etisalat Cash, bank transfers, and cash on delivery. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

How can I speak with a pharmacist?

Call us at +20 (2) 2791-1234, WhatsApp at +20 100 123-4567, email support@pharmacy.eg, or visit our store at 42 Kasr El Nile Street, Downtown Cairo.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and comply with Egyptian healthcare data protection laws. Your privacy is our priority.
